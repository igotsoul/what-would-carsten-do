# "7 Year Anniversary" with Carsten Lützen

## Summary
This is a personal milestone video in which Carsten Lützen marks seven years of posting weekly videos on YouTube and LinkedIn. He shares platform metrics transparently, reflects on his motivations for creating content, and sets intentions for the coming year — including pursuing his PCC certification from ICF.

## Methods & Techniques

### Weekly Video as a Learning Discipline (ab 00:20)
- **What:** Carsten uses weekly video production as a deliberate practice to pace his own learning and develop communication and presentation skills over time.
- **When to use:** When you want a consistent, low-stakes accountability structure for continuous skill development.
- **Group size:** Individual practice
- **Duration:** Ongoing — Carsten has maintained this for 7 years without exception
- **How it works:** Commit to a fixed cadence (weekly), treat each output as a learning artifact rather than a performance, and measure progress over the long arc rather than individual posts.
- **Source:** https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=MKt4LVksJlY&t=20

### Transparent Metrics Sharing (ab 01:10)
- **What:** Carsten publicly shares his platform statistics (followers, views, watch hours, subscriber growth) year-over-year, including when numbers decline.
- **When to use:** When building trust with an audience or modeling intellectual honesty in a coaching/facilitation context.
- **How it works:** Report numbers as-is — growth and decline — without spin. Carsten notes a ~18–28% decline across YouTube metrics and names it plainly rather than avoiding it.
- **Source:** https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=MKt4LVksJlY&t=70

## Key Principles
- **Learning over metrics:** Carsten explicitly frames content creation as a vehicle for his own learning and communication development, not audience growth. This reframe reduces anxiety when numbers decline.
- **Compounding improvement:** He points to measurable growth in presentation and drawing skills over seven years as evidence that consistent practice yields visible results over a long time horizon.
- **Relationships over reach:** The primary value he attributes to his content is the conversations, friendships, and speaking opportunities it generated — not follower counts or view numbers.
- **Curiosity as a driver:** Carsten identifies ongoing curiosity about self-improvement as the engine that keeps the practice alive.
